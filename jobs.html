Our Job Offerings

The BBg Company is always on the lookout for new talent and ambitious, creative and skilled employees. We are a startup business and have fascinating times ahead. Our product will revolutionise how parents of young children see home safety and as a company, we're proud to be part of this development.

We have developed a product that will help prevent injuries in young children and safe lives. Every day, babies and toddlers fall down stairs, burn their little fingers, cut themselves with knives and run on the street. We at BB Company have found the solution to all these problems, the Baby Boom Gate.

Join us today and be part of this revolution. We are a world leader in electronic safety products for children and are dedicated to advancing the technology available to parents. Keeping children safe, With Lasers.

Current Jop Openings

 

 

Electronics Engineer

About the Company

BBG is a company dedicated to the safety of children. We are a world leader in electronic safety products for children and are dedicated to advancing the technology available to parents.

About the Role

As an experienced Electronics Engineer, you will enjoy becoming a part of the family at BBG and working with our team of highly motivated professionals. Your role will be hardware design and construction for child safety products. Reporting directly to the CEO, you will be responsible for:

  • Constructing the Baby Boom Gate safety device
  • Designing new models and advanced features, including hardware and software design, as well as testing
  • Improving safety concerns in any current and future devices to ensure meeting any and all safety requirements
  • Installing devices on site for customers, if required
  • Working with our Brand Marketing Manager to produce technical documentation for our products
Skills and Experience

The successful candidate will have:

  • Tertiary qualifications in Electronic Engineering or relevant field
  • At least 2 years’ experience
  • Self-motivation and initiative
  • Great communication and teamwork
  • Problem solving and troubleshooting skills
  • Knowledge of C programming language is desirable
  • Current Australian driver’s licence

If this is you, we look forward to you joining BBG company to truly make a difference by keeping children safe. With lasers.
