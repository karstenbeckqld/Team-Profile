The BBG Project - Roles, Scope & Limits

Adam is the initiator of the BBG project. He has done the majority of building, coding, and testing of the Baby Boom Gate device. His vision to find a solution to a problem millions of parents face is the driving force for the BBG project. He has the gate in question to be closed in possession. If the device was anywhere else, it could not be tested on the original gate for which the project was designed.

Scope & Limits

The purpose of this project is to create a working prototype using an Arduino to ensure that baby gates can be automatically closed for the safety of children. The project has been scoped for the length of the assignment. As we progress through the specific stages of the project certain deliverables will be produced. By the end of the assignment, we should have the following:

  • Working prototype used for testing and refinement
  • Report on market research to assess financial viability of project
  • Company website and promotional video

The biggest limitation to the project is the amount of time available. Because of this limitation we have decided to create the device as a standalone product that attaches to pre-existing gates, rather than creating our own gate with inbuilt functionality. This significantly reduces the material costs and ensures that we do not have to manufacture our gates to rigid safety standards.

Another limitation to the project is the strength of the stepper motor. As a result of early-stage testing, we found that the stepper motor struggled to close the gate and rather than getting hung up on attempting to figure out how to increase the strength of the motor and introducing mains power, we have opted to investigate a simpler solution of a solenoid that should be able to work a 9V battery. We also discovered issues with the PIR sensor and rather than attempting to fine tune the sensor, which is quite vulnerable to interface, we have opted instead to use a laser and LDR for detection.

Obviously, there are a lot more things that we could do to eventually bring a project like this to the marketplace (as mapped out in our timeline), however due to the time limitations we have kept the scope small enough to be completed within the given period of the assignment.
