Paul Archie

Chief Financial Officer

Hi, my name is Paul Archie (student-ID: s3916672). I was born in Tasmania in 1964 in a small rural community. I completed my schooling in year 10 as that is all the public system went to. I started an apprenticeship in 1980 as a Boilermaker/Welder. I worked for the same company until 1988 when I was made redundant. I moved to Queensland in 1998 to give my sons a better opportunity to make a life for themselves. I had been tending to friends and families IT issues as a hobby up until 2000 when I was forced out of my trade due to an injury. I ran my own IT repair business from 2005 to 2013. In 2019 I decided to go back into business and have been running my own IT mobile repair business since. I find IT is an industry that is only going to grow exponentially, and I enjoy helping clients fix their IT issues. My hobby is Remote Controlled cars. My ideal job is one day being an IT Support Officer.
