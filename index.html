The Baby Boom Gate Company

The BBG Company produces smart devices to improve safety around free to market baby gates of various kinds. We have commited ourselves to provide solutions that will allow parents to have peace of mind when it comes to accidentally left open baby gates.

Baby Safety

The BBG Company works tirelessly on imroving our automated baby gate closer. It doesn't take much for a baby gate to be left open. Our busy daily lives sometimes make us forgetful and we at the BBG Company know that it's never meant intentionally.
So, to give you that extra piece of mind, we have developed the BBG-M1, a sensor triggered gate closer that recognises your little one's movements and shuts an accidentally left open gate automatically.

Parent Safety

We at the BBG Company undertsand that sometimes baby gates are in the way. The temptation to leave them open is only human. As parents and carers ourselves, we often only have one hand avaiable, so, often convenience prevails. And baby gates can become a road block when you have to, for example, move around the weekly shopping or the laundry. So, the BBG Company gives you the freedom to leave a gate open when necessary as we take care of shutting it for you when your little one gets on the move.

The BBG Company developed an understanding for the times when the available technology not always provides satisfactory solutions. Therefore, every team member of the BBG Company works hard to improve current technology and make life for our beloved babies and toddlers safer. To achieve this, we use modern technology like laser diodes and light dependent resistors (LDRs) to make baby gates as safe as possible. 

This way, we can provide Laser tripwires, that can sense when your little one goes on a walkabout when you're not around. The tripwire of course doesn't make your baby trip, it is just a light barrier that can then turn on a little motor mounted to the gate, to close it. So all that's left is to put the tripwire outside your child's door and the motor next to your safety gate. Then if your child leaves their room, they will cross the tripwire and the motor will turn, closing the gate. Now your child is safe, because that's what you want. And that's what we want, at BBG. To protect your child. With lasers.
